Basic Usage

  1. Adding Books
    1. Click on the plus "+" icon beneath the "All Books" section
    2. Add the correct details of any book you are currently reading
    3. Click the "Add" button
  2. Deleting Books
    1. Go to the "All Books" section
    2. Click the "trash can" icon
  3. Favorites
    1. Adding Favorites:  You can add your favorite books to the "Favorites" section by clicking on the hollow star icon found either in the "All Books" or "Recently Read" sections or both
    2. Removing Favorites:  You can remove an item from the "Favorites" section by clicking on the solid star icon found either in the "All Books" or "Recently Read" sections or both; or by clicking on the bookmark icon in the "Favorites Section"
  4. Tracking Study Pace:   Have you ever been in a situation where you are reading a book and it just doesn't seem to end? I'm guessing that's a yes. A really fun way to keep yourself motivated is to keep track of your progress; you can do that by:
    1. Adding the book
    2. Clicking on the view icon (the eye of a shinigami ðŸ˜‚)
    3. Simply check the chapters you've read and the progress you've made is displayed quite colorfully
  5. Recently Read:  Each time you check a chapter of any book, the book is automatically added to the "Recently Read" section. The max items that the said section can hold is five; thus any time you visit the page you can quickly see the last five (could be less) books you read in chronological order
  6. Filtering:  OopsðŸ˜¬ Looking for a book after adding a lot of books? Don't you worry, we've got your back! Type the name of the particular book in the filter input field in the "All Books" section, then do your thing

PS:  If all the guidelines above went over your head, consider reading them again or simply hover over an icon to view its function
